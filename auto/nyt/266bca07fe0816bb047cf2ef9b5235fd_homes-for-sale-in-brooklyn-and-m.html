Homes for Sale in Brooklyn and Manhattan

This week’s properties are in the financial district, Harlem and Brooklyn Heights.

On the Market in New York City

19 Photos

View Slide Show ›

Brad Dickson for The New York Times

Reported by C. J. Hughes and Kim Velsey

  • April 18, 2019

Click on the slide show to see this week’s featured properties:

  • In the financial district, a one-bedroom, one-bath condo with 662 square feet, a living room that can fit a dining-room table, and an open kitchen in a prewar doorman building with a gym.

  • In Harlem, a three-bedroom, two-bath condo with quartz counters in the kitchen, a living room with a balcony, a dining area with built-in banquettes and a guest bedroom with a Murphy bed, in a seven-unit, non-doorman building.

  • In Brooklyn Heights, a spacious one-bedroom apartment with a foyer, four good-sized closets, an open kitchen and a bay-windowed dining nook that looks out onto the Brooklyn Heights promenade, on the first-floor of a full-service co-op built in 1949.
